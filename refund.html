Refund Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with FormSession. If something isn't working as expected, we're happy to help or issue a refund.

1. Free Trial

FormSession includes a 3-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial period. We encourage you to test the extension thoroughly before subscribing.

2. Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund within 14 days of your initial payment if:

  • The extension does not work as described on the websites you need it for
  • You experienced a technical issue that we were unable to resolve
  • You were charged in error

Refund requests made after 14 days will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at alexsheldesign@gmail.com with:

  • Your email address used for purchase
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your order number (found in your Paddle receipt)

We will respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Paddle and typically appear within 5-10 business days.

4. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period. To cancel, visit your Paddle customer portal or contact us.

5. Contact

For refund requests or questions, contact us at alexsheldesign@gmail.com.
