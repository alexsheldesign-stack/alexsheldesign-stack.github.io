FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my form data secure?
Yes. All form data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and never transmitted to any server. Only your subscription status is checked with our backend.
Does it work on all websites?
FormSession works on any website you add to your whitelist. It's specifically optimized for React, Angular, Syncfusion, and other modern JavaScript frameworks where standard autofill typically fails.
What happens when my trial ends?
After the 3-day trial, form saving is paused until you subscribe. Your previously saved sessions remain accessible. You can subscribe for $9.99/month to continue using the extension.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. There are no long-term commitments. After cancellation, you keep access until the end of your billing period.
What browsers are supported?
FormSession is available for Google Chrome and all Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox support is planned for a future release.
